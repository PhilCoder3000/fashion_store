About brand

A celebration of harmony and perfection

With their dedication to harmony and perfect proportions, Filatov is commanding attention within the luxury fashion world for their distinctive approach to design. Inspired by the golden ratio, a mathematical concept most often found in nature, Filatov fuses flawless precision with timeless opulence.

Each Filatov piece embodies the principles of harmony and proportion as portrayed in the golden ratio. Through Filatov’s commitment to excellence, creativity, and innovation, the label aims to set a new standard of luxury that celebrates the beauty of the human form and the interconnection of all living things.

Harmonizing design with Nature’s Golder Ratio

Represented by the letter ‘Ф’, the Filatov name shares its first letter with the symbol for the golden ratio. This emblem serves as a constant reminder of the brand’s devotion to the universe’s invisible governing force. Filatov’s unique design process results in perfection every time, while also paying homage to the ongoing balance found in nature.

Exquisite and meticulously crafted, Filatov is paving the way for a new era of fashion that is unrivaled both in attention to detail and originality.

Our mission

Filatov strives to express the harmony of the golden ratio through their creations. By designing with this principle in mind, Filatov’s pieces celebrate the unique proportions and beauty of every individual while connecting them with the outside world.

Brand values

Filatov is dedicated to excellence on all levels, from the fabrics selected to the connections cultivated with clients and partners. By upholding 4 core values in every aspect of business, Filatov is able to embody the design principles it so highly reveres while providing customers with the best possible experience.

High quality

Filatov only uses the finest materials and employs advanced craftsmanship techniques to ensure each piece created is durable while displaying a stunning level of attention to detail.
